Thai Fried Chicken

Thai Fried Chicken the best fried chicken

Thai fried chicken is a must-try for its perfect balance of crispy texture and bold, aromatic flavors, thanks to a marinade rich in garlic, coriander root, and fish sauce.

unlike typical fried chicken, it offers a unique blend of savory, sweet, and spicy notes that reflect the heart of Thai cuisine.

ingredients

  • Chicken wings or drumsticks
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp coriander root or fresh cilantro stems chopped
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix garlic, coriander, fish sauce, sugar, and pepper into a marinade.
  2. Coat the chicken and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil and deep fry the chicken until golden and crispy.
  4. Drain and serve hot with sweet chili sauce.
